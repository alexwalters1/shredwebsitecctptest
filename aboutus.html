Our mission for inclusivity

This is what we’re about, what we value, and what our goals are. Do these align with your goals? Chat with us!

Values

We believe in inclusivity, expression, and freedom. Our members share these beliefs and teach others the Shred way. Community is critical in making such societal strives, be kind to others, and get everyone involved. The more the merrier!

Vision

We see a future of powerful Shredders, without fear of expression, working with like-minded people and teaching others. Only 23.9% of skateboarders are female, it’s 2024 and this must change.

Goals

Shred is determined to tackle inequality and marginalisation through skateboarding.

1 - An inclusive community for women and those of varying sexual orientation.

2 - Empower women by giving them a safe space to grow.

3 - Shred sexual inequality!
